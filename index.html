About Me

Welcome to my portfolio, where innovation meets creativity! I am a passionate developer dedicated to crafting exceptional apps and websites that deliver seamless functionality and engaging user experiences. With a focus on design and technology, I strive to bring ideas to life through cutting-edge development and creative problem-solving.

Jacob Alexander Figueroa aka Zander is a multifaceted visionary with a remarkable journey that spans art, education, and entrepreneurship. A past student of Full Sail University, he honed his skills in technology and design, building on a solid foundation in music education from the McPhail School of Arts. His diverse experience also includes serving with the Civil Air Patrol, becoming certified to teach English through Oxford University, and spearheading groundbreaking creative spaces.

Zander’s entrepreneurial ventures include founding the Karnak Gallery in Downtown Minneapolis, a hub for avant-garde art and multimedia performances, and launching a music venue in Downtown Los Angeles, which became a celebrated space for emerging talent and live entertainment. His unique blend of artistic innovation, technical expertise, and entrepreneurial spirit continues to inspire and drive creativity in every project he undertakes.
